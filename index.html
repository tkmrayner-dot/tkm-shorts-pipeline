TKM Shorts Pipeline

TKM Shorts Pipeline is a private desktop video automation tool used by the app owner to create, caption, prepare, and upload short-form gaming clips.

What the app does

The app processes the owner’s recorded gaming videos, finds short clip moments, creates vertical short-form videos, generates captions and metadata, and uploads finished clips to the owner’s connected YouTube and TikTok accounts.

How the workflow works

  1. The app owner places an original gaming video into the local input folder.
  2. The local desktop tool analyzes the video and creates short clips.
  3. The tool generates captions, titles, descriptions, and tags for each clip.
  4. The final clips are rendered into vertical short-form videos.
  5. The app uploads the finished videos to the owner’s connected TikTok account using TikTok’s Content Posting API.

TikTok integration

TikTok Login Kit is used only so the app owner can authorize their own TikTok account. The Content Posting API is used only to upload the owner’s own completed gaming clips to the owner’s TikTok account.

Private use only

This app is not a public service. It does not provide accounts, user dashboards, paid services, or public access. It is used only by the app owner for managing the owner’s own video content.

Data handling

The app does not collect data from other TikTok users. It does not sell, share, or transfer personal data. TikTok authorization is used only for account authorization and video uploading by the app owner.

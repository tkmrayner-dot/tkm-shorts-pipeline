TKM Shorts Pipeline

TKM Shorts Pipeline is a private automation tool used by the app owner to create, caption, and upload their own short-form gaming videos to their connected social media accounts.

The app is not available to the public and is used only by the owner. TikTok authorization is used only to upload videos to the owner’s own TikTok account. The app does not collect, sell, or share personal data from other users.

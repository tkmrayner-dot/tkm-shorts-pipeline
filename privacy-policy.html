Overview

TKM Shorts Pipeline is a private desktop automation tool used only by the app owner to process and upload the owner's own gaming video clips.

Information the app uses

  • TikTok authorization tokens provided after the owner authorizes the app.
  • Basic TikTok account information needed to confirm the connected account and posting options.
  • Video files, titles, descriptions, captions, tags, and upload status created by the owner.

How information is used

Information is used only to let the owner authorize their TikTok account and upload the owner's own completed short-form gaming clips through TikTok's Content Posting API.

Information sharing

The app does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties. The app does not collect information from unrelated TikTok users and does not provide public access for other users.

Storage

The desktop workflow stores configuration and authorization information locally on the owner's computer. The app is not a public cloud service and does not operate a public user database.

Data deletion

The owner can remove local app data, generated files, and saved authorization tokens from the local computer. Access can also be revoked through TikTok account settings where applicable.

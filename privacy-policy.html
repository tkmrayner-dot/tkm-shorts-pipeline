TKM Shorts Pipeline Privacy Policy This app is a private automation tool used only by the app owner. The app uses TikTok Login/OAuth only to obtain permission to upload videos to the owner’s TikTok account. The app may store a TikTok access token locally on the owner’s computer for uploading videos. The app does not collect, sell, share, or transfer personal data to third parties. The app does not access other users’ data. To revoke access, the owner can remove the app connection from TikTok account settings.

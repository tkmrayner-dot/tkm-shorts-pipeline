Purpose

TKM Shorts Pipeline is a private desktop tool used by the app owner to create, prepare, and upload short-form gaming clips owned by the app owner.

Private use

This app is not offered as a public service. There are no public user accounts, public dashboards, subscriptions, or third-party upload services.

TikTok authorization

TikTok Login Kit is used only to let the owner authorize the app to access the owner's TikTok account. The Content Posting API is used only to upload the owner's completed video clips to the owner's TikTok account.

Content ownership

The owner is responsible for ensuring that videos, audio, captions, metadata, and uploaded content are allowed to be used and posted.

Acceptable use

  • Do not upload content owned by others without permission.
  • Do not provide upload access to unrelated third parties.
  • Do not violate TikTok policies or other platform rules.
  • Do not collect or misuse data from other users.

No warranty

The tool is provided for private workflow use. Availability, upload behavior, and API features may depend on third-party platform access and approval.
